How it works

Three steps. Live in 14 days.

A ten-minute, mobile-first recording flow. We bring the prompts, the production, and the moderation. You bring the alumni list.

Background, intended major, hometown, financial profile. The right alum reaches the right admit at the right moment in the cycle.

3

Randomized holdout from day one. A defendable, dollar-denominated yield number you can take to the Board in May.
